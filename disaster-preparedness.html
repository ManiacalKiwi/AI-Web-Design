Survival Instructions

How to Clean Water

Ensure you have access to safe drinking water by following these steps:

  1. Boil water vigorously for one minute.
  2. If you cannot boil, use household bleach, 8 drops per gallon of water.
  3. Filter through a clean cloth, paper towel, or coffee filter.
  4. Store treated water in clean, disinfected containers with tight covers.

    5. For a visual guide, watch this tutorial on purifying water.

How to Treat Basic Wounds

Perform first aid on minor wounds using this guide:

  1. Wash your hands with soap and clean water.
  2. Rinse the wound with clean water for at least 5 minutes.
  3. Cover the wound with a sterile bandage or clean cloth.
  4. Apply pressure to stop bleeding.
  5. Seek medical help if the wound is deep, bleeding does not stop, or signs of infection appear.

    6. Check out this tutorial on treating basic wounds for more information.
